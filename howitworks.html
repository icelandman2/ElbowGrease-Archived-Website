Our Mission Statement:

"ElbowGrease was born out of a realization that it is far too difficult for adults and teenagers to find a win-win solution for odd jobs around the house. Gone are the days where you could simply ask the friendly neighborhood paper boy to water your petunias, paint your fence, or walk your dog. Now, we can bring that back. With a simple, elegant design, ElbowGrease enables busy professionals to find eager teenagers to complete tasks that they are simply too busy to do. Adults get peace of mind, and teenagers get to earn for their work on their own schedule. We call that a win-win, and are proud to offer this opportunity to the community."


-The ElbowGrease Team

Goals

How we plan to redefine teenage jobs...

1.

Provide a full, end-to-end system for both employers and employees, all the while simplifying and streamlining the process for all involved. Think faster.

2.

Enable adults to have reliable assistance when needed, without having to worry about the details. Watch in amazement as your tasks simply disappear. Worry about what matters to you. We'll handle the rest.

3.

Ensure quality with our secure "walled garden" system. Currently, only personally verified users are permitted, and all new users must be referred by a current user. This ensures more quality and peace of mind for you. Gone are the days of Craigslist postings.

4.

Provide affordable, reliable service to adults who seek it, and provide regular, well-paying job opportunities to teenagers who need cash. Make free time work better, just a click away.
